Stationeers Cheat Sheet

Alloys

Steel

  • Inputs: Iron x 3, Coal x 1
  • Pressure: Min 1 MPa Max 100 MPa
  • Temperature (k): Min 900 K Max 100kK
  • Temperature (°C): Min 627°C Max 99726°C

Electrum

  • Inputs: Silver x 1, Gold x 1
  • Pressure: Min 1 MPa Max 2.4 MPa
  • Temperature (k): Min 600 K Max 100kK
  • Temperature (°C): Min 327°C Max 99726°C

Invar

  • Inputs: Iron x 1, Nickel x 1
  • Pressure: Min 18 MPa Max 20 MPa
  • Temperature (k): Min 1.2 kK Max 1.5 kK
  • Temperature (°C): Min 927°C Max 1227°C

Constantan

  • Inputs: Copper x 1, Nickel x 1
  • Pressure: Min 20 MPa Max 100 MPa
  • Temperature (k): Min 1 kK Max 10 kK
  • Temperature (°C): Min 727°C Max 9726°C

Solder

  • Inputs: Iron x 1, Lead x 1
  • Pressure: Min 1 MPa Max 100 MPa
  • Temperature (k): Min 350 K Max 550 kK
  • Temperature (°C): Min 77°C Max 277°C

Colours
